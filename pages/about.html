About Pushpa 2 - The Rule

Pushpa 2 is the most awaited movie of the year, continuing the journey of Pushpa, a red sandalwood smuggler. The movie has received massive attention for its action-packed sequences, gripping storyline, and stellar performances.

In this sequel, we explore more of Pushpa's rise and the challenges he faces while navigating through power struggles. The movie features top-notch performances, breathtaking visuals, and an intense soundtrack that keeps the audience on the edge of their seats.
